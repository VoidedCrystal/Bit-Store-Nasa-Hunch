Bit-Store

Bit-Store will store NASA HUNCH projects in a safe and protected environment.

Not Just A Repository

Bit-Store will allow file sharing and version control and perform automated back-ups

Minimal Risk

Unlike USB drives, projects will be accessible from anywhere with no risk of being stolen, lost, or damaged. The only risk we may encounter is databreaches, but we will have security measures in place to prevent that.

Security

Bit-Store will keep project data and user data safe from attackers.
